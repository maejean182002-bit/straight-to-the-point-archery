Our Story & Mission

Founded in 2010, Straight to the Point Archery began with a simple goal: to make the ancient art of archery accessible to everyone in our community. Our mission is to provide a safe, inclusive, and encouraging environment where archers of all levels can sharpen their skills.

Our Facilities

We boast a state-of-the-art indoor range with 20 shooting lanes, climate control for year-round practice, and a fully stocked pro-shop for all your equipment needs.

Range Policies

Safety is our top priority. All archers must follow these core policies:

  • All first-time archers must complete a safety orientation.
  • Closed-toed shoes are required at all times on the range.
  • Follow all whistle commands from the Range Safety Officer.
